About Me

Welcome to my world of miniature artistry, a passion I've been nurturing for the past 30 years. My journey began with a simple goal - to bring color and life to the wargame table through painted figures. Over the years, I've honed my skills to paint with speed, yet never compromising on the aesthetic appeal. My miniatures may not be Golden Demon award winners, but they are crafted to "look good" at arm's length, capturing the essence of their characters and the spirit of the game. I firmly believe that the joy of painting miniatures lies not in the pursuit of perfection, but in creating something that you're happy with and that enhances the gaming experience. So, step into my gallery of painted wargame miniatures, where every figure tells a story and every battle comes to life.
